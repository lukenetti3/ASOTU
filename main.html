How I build websites

1. Schedule a chat

Let’s talk about your business and goals. We will look at the 30k foot view of the websites functionality, pages, and needs to reach your goals. This strategy session is crucial to success.

2. Create a plan

The plan consists of a personalized content template based on your needs. A website's ultimate goal is to serve your customer. You provide the content, and we are on our way.

3. Build, review, launch

Next, we build out your website and hop on a video call to review it. After some tweaks are made, it's launch time.

4. Continued support

We provide the necessary support for continued success with your website. We also offer care plans that continue the relationship beyond website launch.
